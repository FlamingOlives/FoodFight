Neo Clash!

Welcome to Neo Clash, where you use magical spells, curses, and companions to fight your opponents.

  • Go to the Fight tab to practice fighting with other fighters, and earn money to spend in the shop.
  • Go to the Store tab to buy better weapons, armor, shoes, hats, companions, skills, buffs, and more.
  • Go to the Bank tab to manage your money by withdrawing/depositing and looking at interest rates.
  • Go to the Stats tab to check your stats and redeem your achievement points.
